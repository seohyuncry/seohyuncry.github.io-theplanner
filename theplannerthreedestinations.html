FAVOURITES

Finding the right destination for your wedding is key. If you need a wedding planner in Spain to organise your wedding, we are what you are looking for.

  • madrid

    Do you fancy a wedding in the most cosmopolitan city in Spain? Rooftop weddings or estates in the mountains, everything is possible.

  • segovia

    Just an hour away from Madrid you can find the most beautiful towns in Spain, just known for the locals where create a magical wedding.

  • mallorca

    Do you prefer crystalline coves, beach weddings and parties until dawn? Mallorca is your destination. We love having weddings here.

  • menorca

    All the beauty of Mallorca in a smaller more boutique setting. Menorca's charm is discovering the unknown in this Mediterranean paradise.

  • barcelona

    If you can't decide between the seaside or the mountain, in Barcelona, you have it all. Whether in the city or the countryside, you are sure to fall in love with BCN.

  • tarragona

    Are you a wine lover? Then Tarragona it's definitelly a fantastic place for your destination wedding in Spain with one of the best wineries and vineyards regions in Cataluña.

  • seville

    The city of orange trees, flamenco and Guadalquivir river. Unleash your passion in Seville.

  • valencia

    If you dream of a wedding by the sea, Valencia is your destination. The planner recommends a beautiful beach wedding in Valencia.

  • malaga

    Costa del Sol, the perfect location for a destination wedding in Spain

  • toledo

    Get married in the "City of The Three Cultures" will be a dream come true.
